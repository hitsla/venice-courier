1. User Opt-In

Users opt-in by providing their mobile number through our online inquiry forms. By checking the opt-in box, you authorize Venice Courier to send text messages to the mobile number provided. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

2. How to Opt-Out

To stop receiving text messages from Venice Courier, text STOP to the number from which you are receiving messages. You will receive one final confirmation message stating that you have been unsubscribed. After this, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us unless you opt back in.

3. How to Get Help

If you are experiencing issues with the messaging program, you can reply with the keyword HELP for more assistance, or you can reach our support team directly at dispatch@venicecourier.com or call +1 (424) 379-3738.
